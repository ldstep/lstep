About This Site

Posted on September 27, 2024 by Loren

Welcome to my blog! This site is a labor of love.

This blog is built entirely with HTML and CSS. By using static pages, I’ve created a fast, efficient, and secure site that doesn’t rely on complex server-side processing or databases. This site is proudly hosted on GitHub Pages.

This site does not use any tracking scripts, cookies, or analytics tools. Your browsing habits are your own.

By sticking to basic web technologies, this site aims to be accessible to as many devices and browsers as possible.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy the content!
